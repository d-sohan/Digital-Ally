Digital Ally Privacy and Retention Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Processing

Remote AI mode sends the details needed for your request to the Digital Ally proxy and Google Gemini. Local-only mode uses browser templates and makes no calls to the AI proxy or Gemini.

Storage and deletion

Form data and generated content stay in this tab's memory. The browser stores only your processing choice, policy version, and choice date. Digital Ally does not persist prompt or response content on its server. Use “Delete my data” to clear the tab data and saved privacy choice.

Logs

Request and response content is excluded from application logs. Persistent infrastructure logs must contain no user content, use encryption at rest, have restricted access, and be deleted within 30 days.

Consent

Remote processing requires an explicit, versioned choice. You may switch to local-only mode or delete your data at any time.
